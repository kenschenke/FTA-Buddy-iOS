Welcome to FTA Buddy

This app is for volunteers at FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) events in the FTA (FIRST Technical Adviser) or FTAA (FIRST Technical Adviser Assistant) roles. It's a small app that serves three main purposes: display the field monitor in an embedded browser, show flashcards (like the FTA Notepad app), and show a quick reference page with some helpful technical tidbits.

Note: this app was developed entirely by me with no input or cooperation with FIRST. FIRST had nothing do with this app, so no endorsement or implicit approval is implied. If you want to know more information about FIRST, visit their web site at www.firstinspires.org.

Cookies and Tracking

FTA Buddy does not use any cookies. Web sites you visit with the embedded browser might, but that's not FTA Buddy doing that. The app doesn't track user behavior at all. No analytics are collected and the app doesn't "phone home".
